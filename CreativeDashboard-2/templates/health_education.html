Essential Health Tips for Rural Communities
Water & Sanitation
  • Boil water for 10 minutes before drinking
  • Wash hands with soap frequently
  • Use clean toilets and proper drainage
  • Keep food covered and fresh
Prevention is Better
  • Get regular health check-ups
  • Complete vaccination schedules
  • Eat nutritious local foods
  • Exercise regularly and get adequate sleep
Warning Signs - Seek Help
  • High fever for more than 3 days
  • Severe diarrhea or vomiting
  • Difficulty breathing or chest pain
  • Unusual bleeding or severe injuries
Community Health
  • Share health knowledge with neighbors
  • Support pregnant women and new mothers
  • Keep your environment clean
  • Report health issues to ASHA workers
